PROJECT DEPARTMENT

A project management office , government agency, or enterprise that defines and maintains standards for project management within the organization. The PMO strives to standardize and introduce economies of repetition in the execution of projects. The PMO is the source of documentation, project management and execution.

QUALITY DEPARTMENT

Quality management ensures that an organization, product or service consistently functions well. It has four main components: quality planning, quality assurance, quality control and quality improvement. Quality control is also part of quality management. What a customer wants and is willing to pay for it, determines quality.
